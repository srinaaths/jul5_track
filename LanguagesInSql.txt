4 types:
DDL:
Includes creating and altering the schema of the database
Eg. CREATE, DROP, ALTER, TRUNCATE

DML
Includes updating the data in the database
Eg. SELECT, UPDATE, INSERT, DELETE

DCL
Includes commands for access to the database 
Eg. GRANT, REVOKE

TCL
commands used to manage the transactions in the DB - COMMIT, ROLLBACK
